Sodium Bicarbonate

Approved for Advanced CCEMS units only

Protocol 1010

NOT a treatment protocol. See reference protocol listed below for use.

Reference Protocols: 102, 103, 508, 511, 707, 717, 724.

Class: Alkalinizing Agent

MECHANISM OF ACTION

1. Neutralizes excess acids.
2. Promotes TCA excretion by alkalinizing urine.

INDICATIONS
1. Prolonged CPR after ventilation, Defibrillation, Epinephrine, and Lidocaine or Amiodarone.
2. Tricyclic Antidepressant Overdose.
3. Known Hyperkalemia (Severe DKA).

CONTRAINDICATIONS
1. Respiratory Acidosis

SIDE EFFECTS
1. Metabolic Alkalosis if given in large doses.

Dosage and Administration

Contact Medical Control Prior to Administration

Adult Dose:

Cardiac Arrest: 1 mEq/kg IV/IO

1. Use early if pre-existing metabolic acidosis, hyperkalemia, tricyclic antidepressant OD.
2. Prolonged resuscitation if ventilation is adequate.
3. Return of spontaneous circulation after long period of arrest.

Toxic Ingestion: 1 mEq/kg Slow IV Push
1.IV/IO push for tricyclic antidepressant overdose with sustained HR >120 bpm and QRS >0.1 seconds.
2.Hypotension unresponsive to fluids.
3. Ventricular Dysrhythmias.

High Voltage Electrical Injury or Direct Lightning Strike with Significant Tissue Destruction
1. Sodium Bicarbonate 1mEq/kg (Maximum of 100 mEq) in 1000mL NS open wide.

Crush Injuries: Must Contact OLMC prior to administration
1. Constant crush injuries greater than 30 minutes duration and if the patient develops cardiac arrythmias or a prolonged QRS >100mS:
Adminster Sodium Bicarbonate 1mEq/kg (maximum of 100 mEq) IV push prior to the beginning of infusion up to TWO additional times plus start Sodium Bicarbonate Drip 1mEq/kg (Maximum of 100mEq) in 1000mL NS and run wide open.

Pediatric Dose

Children >1 Month
1. Sodium Bicarbonate 1 mEq/kg of 8.4% solution

Children <1 Month
1. Sodium Bicarbonate 0.5 mEq of 4.2% solution

Cardiac Arrest, Toxic Ingestion, High voltage electrical injury or direct lightning strike with significant tissue destruction, crush injuries
1. Not a first line drug. Contact OLMC for permission.
2. Same indications as adult.

CONSIDERATIONS

1. FLUSH LINE WELL AFTER USE.
