Lasix (Furosemide)

Approved for Rescue Paramedics Only

Protocol 1014

NOT a treatment protocol. See reference protocol listed below for use.

Reference Protocols: 402.

Class: Hormone - Diuretic

MECHANISM OF ACTION

1. Inhibits sodium/chloride ion reabsorption in the kidney causes venous dilation.
2. Onset within 5 minutes of administration.

INDICATIONS
1. Congestive Heart Failure.
2. Pulmonary Edema.

CONTRAINDICATIONS
1. Pregnancy.

SIDE EFFECTS
1. Dysrhythmias.
2. Hypotension.
3. Electrolyte Imbalances - Medical control option - OLMC must be contacted prior to administration.

Dosage and Administration

Adult: 40-80 mg (standard IV dose is the same as the patient's oral dose)

Pediatric: 1mg/kg (Max Dose 40mg).

CONSIDERATIONS

1. Give slow IV push.
2. Use caution in presence of digoxin or lithium use.
