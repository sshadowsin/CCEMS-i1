SYNCOPE

Any patient, with transient loss (or near loss) of consciousness.

EXCLUSIONS

  1. Altered mental status--use Protocol 306: Altered Level of Consciousness
  2. Criteria for other specific Protocol,such as shock, arrythmia or head trauma.

EMT

  1. PROTECT the patient from physical harm and embarrassment.
  2. ASSESS LEVEL OF CONSCIOUSNESS and maintain AIRWAY as indicated.
  3. Place patient in left lateral recumbent position to prevent aspiration unless MOIindicates possibility of spinal injury then immobilize appropriately.
  4. SUCTION as necessary.
  5. OXYGEN via NC 4-6 L or face mask at 15L as needed to maintain pulse ox >94%

ADVANCED EMT

  1. APPLY ECG MONITOR - obtain 12 Lead and transmit for evaluation.
  2. SALINE IV @ TKO rate unless hypotensive then administer bolus 250-500 mf or saline lock.
  3. CHECK GLUCOMETER READING _ IF BEKOW 60 mg/dl, give Dextrose D50 25 GRAMS IV PUSH. If no IV access after TWO attempts. consider Glucagon 1 mg IM.
  4. CONSULT OLMC.
  5. TRANSPORT. Monitor ECG, vitals and neurological status.

PARAMEDIC

  1. ASSIST VENTILATION
  2. Insert Advanced airway as applicable.
  3. Look for underlying causes, hypovolemia, dysrhythmia, toxic ingestion, neurological crisis.
