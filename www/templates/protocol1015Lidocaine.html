Lidocaine

Approved for Advanced CCEMS Units Only

Protocol 1015

NOT a treatment protocol. See reference protocol listed below for use.

Reference Protocols: 102, 202, 300, 401, 502, 505, 710, 711, 718.

Class: Hormone - Antiarrhythmic

MECHANISM OF ACTION

1. Supresses ventricular ectopic activity by decreasing excitability of the myocardium.
2. Increases Fibrillation threshold.
3. Can prevent recurrence of V-fib/V-tach in converted patients.

POSSIBLE INDICATIONS PENDING MEDICAL CONTROL APPROVAL
1. Ventricular Tachycardia.
2. Ventricular Fibrillation.
3. Wide complex tachyarrhythmia of uncertain cause.

CONTRAINDICATIONS
1. Allergy to Lidocaine or Novacaine.
2. Heart rate <60.
3. 2nd or 3rd Degree Heart Block.
4. PEA or Asystole.

SIDE EFFECTS
1. Hypotension.
2. Bradycardia.
3. High doses may cause seizures.

Dosage and Administration

Adult Dose IV/IO:
1. Refractory VFIB/VTACH: 1mg/kg IV/IO Every 3-5 minutes to Max 3mg/kg.
2. VTach with Pulse: 1mg/kg IV/IO every 3-5-10 minutes to Max 3mg/kg.
3. Criteria PVC's (Suspected Acute Coronary Syndrome with >6 PVC's per minute, couplets, runs of VTACH, multifocal PVC's): 1mg/kg IV
4. Initial bolus should be followed with Lidocaine 0.5mg/kg doses every 10 minutes to a total dose of 3mg/kg.

Pediatric Dose IV/IO:
1.VFIB or VTACH WITHOUT a pulse: 1mg/kg IV or IO Every 3-5 inutes to MAX 3mg/kg.
2. VTACH with a Pulse: 1mg/kg IV/IO every 3-5 minutes to max 3mg/kg.

Bronchospasm/Wheezing: Contact OLMC prior to administration

1. Lidocaine 2% 40-60mg (2-3 mL) added to Albuterol for adult patients with "cough variant asthma" which is severe coughing which inhibits respiratory function (with or without audible wheezes).

ADJUNCT TO INTUBATION WITH CLOSED HEAD INJURY

1. Lidocaine 1mg/kg IV/IO 60-90 seconds prior to intubation (if IV access readily available).

ADJUNCT TO INFUSION IN A COINSCIOUS PATIENT

Prior to running fluids administer 40mg (2mL) of 2% Lidocaine thru the IO and flush with 10mL of NS, wait 15 seconds and administer IV fluid

Considerations
1. If Lidocaine is the first drug used then all repeat doses shall be with Lidocaine. Never alternate between Lidocaine and Amiodarone .
2. In cardiac arrest elevate arm and flush after each dose.
3. Obtain 12 lead ECG - continuous cardiac monitoring.
4.After successful conversion of cardiac arrest due to VFIB/VTACH, a Lidocaine drip may be started (use Dial-A-Flow to regulate drip), an initial bolus should be given prior to starting a drip.
