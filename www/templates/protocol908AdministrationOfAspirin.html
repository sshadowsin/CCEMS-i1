Administration of Aspirin

This protocol is for the EMT First Responder only.

Determine Need for Aspirin:

  1. Refer to Protocol 300: CHEST PAIN (ACUTE CORONARY SYNDROME)
  2. Patient has chest pain
  3. Obtain order from online medical control to administer aspirin per protocol

Check for Contraindications:

  1. Pregnancy
  2. Bleeding disorders
  3. Peptic ulcers
  4. Use of anticoagulants
  5. Hypersensitivity or allergy to aspirin

Inquire About Previous Doses:

  1. Total doses taken/administered
  2. When last dose taken/administered
  3. Effects of previous dose
  4. Any other interventions

Obtain aspirin- Ensure the “six rights” of medication administration:

  1. Right patient
  2. Right medication
  3. Right dose
  4. Right route
  5. Right time
  6. Right documentation
  7. Check expiration date
  8. Check if medication is discolored

Contact OLMC prior to administration for order

Administer Aspirin:

  1. Have patient chew, not swallow (4) 81 mg chewable aspirin or one 325 mg adult aspirin
  2. Reassess patient's response to medication
