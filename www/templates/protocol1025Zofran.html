Zofran

Ondansetron
Approved for Rescue/Ambulance Units Only

Protocol 1025

NOT a treatment protocol. See reference protocol listed below for use.

Reference Protocols: 509.

Anti Nausea / Antiemetic

Mechanism of Action:

Zofran Works to Prevent Nausea and Vomiting by Blocking Chemoreceptors in the CNS and Peripheral Nervous System.

INDICATIONS

1. Nausea and Vomiting.
2. Adjunt to Narcotic Administration (Common Side Effect of any Narcotic is Nausea.)
3. Motion Sickness.

CONTRAINDICATIONS

1. Hypersensitivity to this Drug.
2 Use Cautiously with Hepatic Impairment.

Dosage and Administration

Adult Dose

1. 4mg IV May Repeat x1 in 5-10 Min x1 Dose.
2. 8mg ODT May be Given x1 dose. If no Relief in 20 Minutes, Give 4mg Zofran IV x1 Dose.

Peds Dose

1. 2mg IV May Repeat x1 in 5-10 Minutes.
2. 4mg (1/2 of an 8mg ODT) May be Given.
