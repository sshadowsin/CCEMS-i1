Tylenol Elixir

Children's Acetaminophen
Approved for Advanced CCEMS Units Only

Protocol 1022

NOT a treatment protocol. See reference protocol listed below for use.

Reference Protocols: 716

Antipyretic

Acetaminophen - 160mg / 5mL

INDICATIONS

A Temperature of 101 Degrees (39.0 C) Must be Documented Prior to Administering any Fever Control Medication. If the Patient is Awake and Able to Maintain Their Airway Without Assistance Then the Following Steps May be Instituted.

1. Remove Extra Clothing - Keep in T-Shirt / Diaper.
2. Prevent Overheating by Not Wrapping in Blankets.
3. Provide Fever Control Medications Appropriate to Age.
4. Advanced EMTs Shall Contact On-Line Medical Control Prior to Administration of Any Medication.

Dosage and Administration

DOSAGE / ADMINISTRATION: USUAL DOSE IS 15MG/KG

1. Child's Tylenol Shall be Given if it Has Not Been Administered Within the Last 4 Hours.
(Children's Tylenol Normal Dosing is q 4 hours).
