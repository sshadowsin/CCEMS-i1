NEEDLE DECOMPRESSION PROCEDURE

Paramedic Only

INDICATIONS:

Patients with signs of PNEUMOTHORAX associated with SHOCK or HYPOTENSION.

Protocol:

  1. ORGANIZES necessary equipment.
    a. Alcohol/iodine wipes.
    b. Angio catheter 12-16 ga. (longest length available).
    c. 10cc syringe.
    d. Tape.
  2. Find LANDMARKS- 2nd intercostals space, mid-clavicular line on the affected side.
  3. Insert needle over top of 3rd rib at 90- degree angle.
  4. ASPIRATE for air while advancing needle.
  5. After entering pleura and getting air return, remove syringe and needle.
  6. CHECK for air movement.
  7. Securely tape catheter.
  8. REASSESS breath sounds every 2-3 minutes.
