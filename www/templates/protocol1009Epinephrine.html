EPINEPHRINE

Approved for Advanced CCEMS units only

Protocol 1009

NOT a treatment protocol. See reference protocol listed below for use.

Reference Protocols: 101, 102, 103, 104, 105, 201, 302, 303, 304, 310, 311, 312, 313, 314, 315, 318, 401, 500, 502, 503, 504, 602, 609, 702, 704, 707, 709.

Class: Cardiac Stimulant

MECHANISM OF ACTION

1. Increases Heart Rate
2. Increases Cardiac Contractile Force
3. Increases Electrical Activity in the Myocardium
4. Increases Blood Pressure
5. Lowers Threshold for Defibrillation

INDICATIONS
1. Cardiac Arrest (Asystole, VF, VT, PEA) - (EPI 1:10,000)
2. Severe Anaphylaxis (EPI 1:1,000)
3. Severe Reactive Airway Disease (EPI 1:1,000)
4. Give EPI 1:1000 SQ ONLY for allergic reactions, asthma

CONTRAINDICATIONS:

Cardiac Arrest: NONE

SIDE EFFECTS
1. Hypertension
2. Increase Myocardial Oxygen Consumption
3. Nausea/Vomiting

Dosage and Administration

Cardiac Arrest

ADULT DOSE:
1. EPI 1:10,000 Solution 1.0mg IV/IO
2. Repeat every 3-5 minutes throughout the code.
3. After each dose by peripheral IV - Flush IV line with 20mL of NS and elevate arm to ensure delivery to the central circulation.

PEDIATRIC DOSE:
1. 0.01mg/kg (0.1mL/kg) of 1:10,000 solution IV/IO (Max dose 1mg)
2. NEWBORN RESUSCITAION ONLY: Medical control option- Must contact prior to administration
a. 0.01-0.03 mg/kg (0.1-0.3 mL/kg) of 1:10,000 IV/IO
b. Repeat every 3-5 minutes until spontaneous heart rate remains >60bmp.

Acute Allergic Reaction/Acute Asthma/Anaphylaxis

ADULT DOSE:
1. 0.3mg of 1:1,000 Solution SQ x1 dose. Contact OLMC for additional dosage instructions

PEDIATRIC DOSE:
1. 0.01 mg/kg of 1:1,000 Solution SQ up to 0.3mg x1 dose. Contact OLMC for additional dosage instructions.

Suspected Croup/Stridor

Epinephrine (1:1,000) 2mL mixed with 3 mL of NS via nebulizer.

CONSIDERATIONS

1. Protect from light, discard if solution is not clear
2. DO NOT administer with bicarb, it will be deactivated
3. Monitor vital signs closely when given SQ

Paramedic Only: EPI Drip

1.Epinephrine 2-10mcg/min
2.Start at 2mcg/min
3.Titrate systolic BP until it reaches 90-100 mmHg
4.Mix Epinehprine 2mg of 1:1000 in 1000mL NS which yields 0.002 mg/mL.
5. Use Dial-a-flow extension set
