PEDIATRIC CARDUAC TAMPONADE (SUSPECTED)

INDICATIONS:

  1. Patents with a systolic blood pressure less than expected for age, who are dyspneic and/or tachypneic with:
    • Diminished or absent heart sounds.
    • Obvious distended neck veins.
    • Clearly auscultated bilateral breath sounds.

EMT

  1. Focused history and physical exam.
  2. Obtain a full set of Vital Signs including blood glucose.
  3. Provide basic airway maneuvers to all compromised airways, i.e. oxygen, jaw-thrust, and positioning.
  4. Provide 100% oxygen to the patient.
  5. Ventilate with BVM when apneic or exhibiting respiratory disress. Consider a nasal or oral airway.
  6. Recommended pediatric ventilatory rates:
    • Infant (0-12 month): 25 breaths per minute.
    • 1-3 years: 20 breaths per minutes.
    • 4-6 years: 15 breths per minute.
    • >6 years: 12 (same as adult).
  7. Do not hyperventilate the patient maintain an oxygen saturation 92-94%.

ADVANCED EMT

  1. Ensure above is complete.
  2. Continuous ECG, ETCO2, and Pulse Oximetry monitoring when available.
  3. Immediate TRANSPORT - consult EMS physician.
  4. Establish vascular access x2 if able.
  5. Run wide open under pressure NS 20 ml/kg if tachycardia or signs of poor perfusion- repeat up to max 60 ml/kg.
  6. If no signs of poor perfusion, no tachycardia and no hypotension, run IV at TKO.

PARAMEDIC:

  1. Ensure the above is complete.
  2. INTUBATION- Consider orotracheal intubation using an endotracheal tube when indicated.
  3. Document TWO confirmation methods to varify endotracheal placement of ET tube (e.g. EtCO2, CO2 Detection Device or Esophogeal Intubation Detector.
  4. No nasotracheal intubation.

Medical Control Option-Must contact prior to administration.

  1. Sedation after intubation (benzodiazepine).
  2. Treat for pain and anxiety, consider Protocol 509: Pain Control/Nausea/Vomiting/Anxiety and contact OLMC for medication order:
    • Fentanyl 1 mcg/kg (max 25 mcg per dose) IV/IO.
    • In the event an IV cannot be established, Fentanyl 1 mcg/kg may be given IM up an initial dose of 25mcg.
    • For additional doses, contact OLMC.
